Is your child or someone you know struggling with academics?

You may be spending extra time helping your child or have tried tutoring with little or no success. The problem could be weak cognitive skills – the skills that form the foundation for learning. A cognitive skills assessment can identify whether this is the case. Learning can be difficult if the underlying skills are not developed.

Cognitive skills like attention, working memory, logic and reasoning, and processing speed need to be developed for a student to be able to take in information, understand it, and quickly use it. Cognitive training builds these underlying skills that must be in place for learning to occur.

ACE Brain Fitness provides one-on-one training focused on the student's personal needs. A cognitive skills assessment identifies the areas of greatest concern and these skills are then strengthened through an individualized brain fitness program.

Have you or has someone you know suffered from a traumatic brain injury?

Some common symptoms after undergoing a traumatic brain injury include memory loss, lack of focus, poor problem solving and decision making, poor reading comprehension, and difficulty with organization and multi-tasking. Research has shown that cognitive training can significantly improve the ability of patients with chronic brain injury to maintain attention on goals and execute tasks – skills these patients often lack as a result of their injuries.

If you recognize these symptoms in yourself or someone else, get professional help.

ACE Brain Fitness offers training that focuses on skills weakened by a traumatic brain injury.

Do you have a student that will be taking the ACT test?

Getting a competitive score on the ACT test can be essential to getting into the college of choice, as well as improving the chance of qualifying for a scholarship.

ACE Brain Fitness offers a skill building/ACT prep course designed to maximize potential through a unique combination of cognitive skills training along with practical ACT test-taking techniques.
